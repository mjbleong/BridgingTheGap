Our Mission

We are a group of four Stanford undergrads working on a platform to connect high school students with college-going alumni from their local communities. We hope that by bridging the communication gap between these groups, high school students can begin their college journeys with more preparation for the challenges they may face, and the support of belonging to a wider community of college-attending alumni.
